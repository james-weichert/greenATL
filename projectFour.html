Unfortunately, there aren't enough non-profit organization or initiatives to provide for the greenification of entire metro Atlanta. Therefore, we rely on self- and community-driven initiatives to greenify neighborhoods throughout Atlanta. It just takes the willingness to get out in your community and make a change for the better. These greenification initiatives can be as large as advocating for or developing small designated greenspaces within your community or neighborhood, or even planting a few trees around your house.


To find out more about how you can greenify your community, watch our Campaign Video, check out our "Get Involved" Section, or follow us on Social Media for updates on local volunteer opportunities as well as inspiration for community greenification projects.
