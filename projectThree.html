A city within a city, Buckhead is a bustling neighborhood with skyscrapers, lavish residential neighborhoods, trendy restaurants and unique botiques. What's more? - It has its own share of parks and greenspaces; from Chastain park to Atlanta Memorial Park to small Loudermilk Park in the heart of Downtown Buckhead. And with greenification and urbanization projects like Path 400 and Buckhead Redefined, Buckhead is promising to become a truly modern and greenified area of the city.


To find out more about Buckhead and how you can help to shape its development, check out BuckheadRedefined.com.
