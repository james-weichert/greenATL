Although the Beltline has only been around for a few years, it has quickly been adopted by Atlanta as an integral part of the modern city. The Beltline, a 22 mile-long urban walking/biking trail, which spans dozens of metro Atlanta neighborhoods, has truly connected Atlanta and has ignited the city's passion for walking, biking, running and other outdoor activities. It connects Piedmont Park to Midtown, to Inman Park, and to Buckhead. And the best part? -It's still growing; the Beltline initiative has plans to expand the Beltline throughout the entire city, truly connecting Atlanta through its citizens' love for greenspace.


To find out more about the Beltline and how you can participate in its growth and development, check out Beltline.org.
